# ⚖️ Legal & Privacy Requirements

## Privacy Policy (REQUIRED)

Your app collects data and has subscriptions, so you MUST have a privacy policy.

### Required Disclosures:

- **Data Collection**:

  - Game statistics and achievements (stored locally)
  - Subscription status and payment information
  - App usage analytics (if you add analytics)
  - TMDB API usage for movie data

- **Third-Party Services**:

  - The Movie Database (TMDB) API
  - Apple App Store / Google Play Store (for subscriptions)
  - Any analytics services you add

- **Data Storage**:
  - Local storage using AsyncStorage
  - No server-side data collection (currently)

### Template Privacy Policy:

```
CineMaze Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Date]

1. Information We Collect:
   - Game progress and statistics (stored locally on your device)
   - Subscription and payment information (processed by Apple/Google)
   - Movie preferences and watchlist (stored locally)

2. How We Use Information:
   - To provide game functionality and track progress
   - To manage subscriptions and premium features
   - To improve the app experience

3. Third-Party Services:
   - We use The Movie Database (TMDB) for movie information
   - Apple App Store/Google Play Store handle all payment processing
   - We do not share personal information with third parties

4. Data Security:
   - All game data is stored locally on your device
   - We use industry-standard security for any transmitted data

5. Contact Us:
   - Email: [your-email@example.com]
```

## Terms of Service (REQUIRED)

### Must Include:

- Subscription terms (auto-renewal, cancellation)
- Acceptable use policy
- Intellectual property rights
- Limitation of liability
- Governing law

## App Store Compliance

### iOS Requirements:

- Privacy Policy linked in App Store Connect
- In-app purchase terms clearly disclosed
- Subscription auto-renewal clearly explained
- Family Sharing compatibility (optional)

### Android Requirements:

- Privacy Policy linked in Google Play Console
- Target SDK version 34+ (check your current version)
- Data safety form completed in Play Console
